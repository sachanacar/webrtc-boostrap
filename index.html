Welcome to WebRTC Boostrap! The crowdsourced starter-kit and “WebRTC dictionary” to help you build and scale your WebRTC porjects. This is a reading-friendly WebRTC starter kit for developers by developers. It includes a list of vendors/SDKs for all the necessary steps and a WebRTC dictionary with all the terms needed to get familiar with WebRTC development and help you bootstrap your way through deploying your WebRTC solution. Check out the Starter Kit page and the WebRTC Dictionary page!
